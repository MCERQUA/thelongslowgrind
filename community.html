Frequently Asked Technical Specs

How often are the workshop recordings updated?

add
Workshop recordings are uploaded within 48 hours of each live session. We run 2-4 workshops per month, covering topics from estimating and bidding to crew management and business development. All recordings are archived and searchable in your member dashboard.

Can I upgrade my tier mid-billing cycle?

add
Absolutely. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll only be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.

Is mentorship available for sub-contractors?

add
Yes. Our mentorship program is designed for anyone in the trades, whether you're a general contractor, specialty sub, or owner-operator. The Master tier includes 1-on-1 mentorship sessions tailored to your specific trade and business stage.

What is the 'Safety Manual' protocol for the forums?

add
The Safety Manual is our community code of conduct. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for spam, self-promotion without value, and disrespect. This is a professional network -- every interaction should add structural integrity to the community, not tear it down.
