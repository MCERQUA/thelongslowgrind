01 / THE INFLECTION POINT

The world is facing a long slow burn. Across the trades, we're seeing the fallout of a massive labor shortage--a generation of skills fading without heirs.

But for most contractors, the shortage isn't the primary enemy. The enemy is the "busy being busy" cycle. You're working 80 hours a week, putting out fires, and chasing invoices. You aren't building a legacy; you're building a cage. We saw the grind and decided it was time to change the gear.

The Mission

"WE EXIST TO HELP CONTRACTORS ESCAPE THE GRIND OF WORKING IN THEIR BUSINESS AND START WORKING ON IT."
